Indiana's season took a devastating turn when Tyrese Haliburton went down with an Achilles injury during the Finals last spring. The Pacers have been fighting through adversity all year, but their offense has never quite found the same rhythm without their All-Star point guard.

Orlando's defensive identity has been elite this season. Paolo Banchero is making his case as the league's best young player, and the Magic's length causes problems for everyone. Their home-court advantage has been significant.

Brooklyn is in full tank mode, but they play hard every night. The Nets' young players are competing for roster spots, which makes them dangerous against teams that might overlook them on the schedule.

Denver should win this game, but the spread feels tight. The Nuggets are still elite defensively, and their depth has stepped up this season. Take Denver to cover on the road, but don't be surprised if it's closer than expected.
