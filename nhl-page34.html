Jets @ Leafs

2026-01-02T00:00Z | Scotiabank Arena | NHL Net
Line: TOR -135 | O/U: 6.5 | WPG: 15-19-4 | TOR: 18-15-6

You know what's funny about home ice advantage? Sometimes it's just lipstick on a pig.

Toronto's got the friendly bounces and last change, sure, but look at these numbers - both teams are sitting at a big fat zero percent in their respective venues lately. The Leafs are 4-6 in their last ten at Scotiabank, which sounds mediocre until you realize Winnipeg's managed exactly ONE road win in their last ten tries. One!

Here's the thing about home cooking - it matters most when teams are evenly matched. But when you've got a road squad that's basically allergic to winning away from home facing a home team that's been sleepwalking through their own building, the advantage becomes real. Toronto's problems are mostly effort-based; Winnipeg's road woes run deeper than that.

The Jets are broken on the road right now, and even a mediocre home team can feast on that kind of psychological damage. Scotiabank Arena might not be rocking, but it beats whatever mental cemetery the Jets drag around with them.

**Take the Leafs -135.**
