Tennessee Volunteers vs Rutgers Scarlet Knights

1:00 PM ET TNT Las Vegas

Baylor Bears vs Creighton Bluejays

2:00 PM ET truTV

Two programs with Final Four aspirations clash in an early afternoon showcase. Baylor's physicality meets Creighton's shooting prowess in what should be a back-and-forth affair.

Bill Self's Jayhawks look to establish early season momentum against a Notre Dame team seeking statement wins. Kansas traditionally dominates early season showcases with their depth and execution.
