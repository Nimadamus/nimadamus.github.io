The San Antonio Spurs bring their promising young core to Madison Square Garden to face the New York Knicks in an NBA Cup showcase game. Victor Wembanyama continues to demonstrate why he was the consensus first overall pick, averaging impressive numbers across the board while anchoring the Spurs' defense. His combination of rim protection and perimeter skills has San Antonio believing they've found their next franchise cornerstone.

The Knicks have been rock solid under Tom Thibodeau, playing the tough, physical brand of basketball that defines New York. Jalen Brunson has emerged as one of the league's most reliable point guards, and the supporting cast has stepped up when needed. MSG always brings an extra level of energy for primetime games, and the Knicks will look to use that home court advantage to stifle the Spurs' young offensive talent.
