Bulls @ Hawks

2025-12-21T20:30Z | State Farm Arena | CHSN, NBA League Pass
Line: ATL -5.5 | O/U: 247.5 | CHI: 12-15 | ATL: 15-14

Two teams in the middle of the Eastern Conference pack square off in Atlanta. The Hawks at 15-14 have been inconsistent but dangerous at home, while Chicago at 12-15 is still trying to find their identity this season. Trae Young remains one of the most electrifying playmakers in the league, and he's been cooking at State Farm Arena.

The Bulls have struggled on the road, particularly against teams with explosive guards. Chicago's perimeter defense has been a liability all season, and that plays right into Atlanta's hands. The Hawks' offense generates open looks at a high rate, and Young is too good to let those opportunities go to waste.

Atlanta laying 5.5 at home feels about right. The Hawks have covered this number in four of their last six home games, and Chicago doesn't have the defensive identity to slow them down. Take Atlanta to win by 6+.

Raptors @ Nets

Line: TOR -3.5 | O/U: 215.5 | TOR: 17-12 | BKN: 7-19

Brandon Ingram has been outstanding since arriving in Toronto, giving the Raptors exactly the star power they needed. Paired with Scottie Barnes, they have a two-way duo that can compete with anyone. The Nets simply don't have the personnel to match up defensively.

Toronto laying 3.5 on the road against a tanking team is a gift. The Raptors have been covering spreads consistently against below-.500 teams, and Brooklyn's porous defense gives up 120+ regularly. Take Toronto to cruise and focus on their playoff positioning.

Heat @ Knicks
