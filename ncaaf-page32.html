Tigers @ Wolfpack

2025-12-19T19:30Z | Raymond James Stadium | ESPN
MEM: 8-5 | NCSU: 8-5

The Gasparilla Bowl features two programs with identical 8-5 records looking to finish strong. Memphis finished the American Athletic Conference season on a high note, with their explosive offense averaging 32.1 points per game. The Tigers' passing attack can stretch any defense vertically.

NC State had a solid regular season in the ACC despite losing close games to some of the conference's elite. The Wolfpack's defense is physical and well-coached, which should make this an interesting stylistic matchup at Raymond James Stadium.

This game comes down to whether NC State's defense can slow Memphis's tempo. Look for a competitive affair that stays close throughout, with the team that wins the turnover battle likely walking away victorious.

Bulldogs @ Panthers

2025-12-13T17:00Z | Mercedes-Benz Stadium | ABC
SCST: 10-3 | PV: 10-4

The HBCU national championship matchup at Mercedes-Benz Stadium features two elite programs. South Carolina State's 10-3 record represents another excellent season for the Bulldogs, who have dominated the MEAC and earned their spot on this stage.

Prairie View A&M enters with a 10-4 mark and has been one of the best teams in the SWAC all season. The Panthers have explosive playmakers on offense and a defense that's gotten better as the season progressed. The Atlanta crowd will be electric for this showcase.

This is HBCU football at its finest. Both teams play with tremendous pride and passion, and this game should deliver plenty of excitement. The team with the better defensive performance in big moments will likely hoist the trophy.

Knights @ Midshipmen

2025-12-13T20:00Z | M&T Bank Stadium | CBS
ARMY: 6-6 | NAVY: 10-2

America's Game at M&T Bank Stadium. There's no rivalry quite like Army-Navy, where records go out the window and every play matters. Army enters at 6-6, having struggled for consistency this season but knowing that a win here erases all the frustration. The Black Knights' triple option can control the clock when it's clicking.

Navy is having a tremendous season at 10-2 and comes in as the heavy favorite. The Midshipmen's option attack has been devastating, and their defense has improved significantly. They'll be fired up to cap a special season with a win over their biggest rival.

Throw out the records in this one. Both teams will play their hearts out for bragging rights that last an entire year. Navy has the better team on paper, but Army has nothing to lose and will play with reckless abandon. Expect a low-scoring, physical classic.

Broncos @ Huskies

2025-12-14T01:00Z | SoFi Stadium | ABC
BOIS: 9-5 | WASH: 9-4

Boise State brings their high-powered offense to SoFi Stadium. The Broncos finished 9-5 with a strong Mountain West showing, led by their dynamic quarterback and a rushing attack that can gash anyone. The famous blue turf magic travels well when Boise is locked in.

Washington went 9-4 in a transitional year following their national championship game appearance. The Huskies are rebuilding but still have talent, particularly in their secondary. Playing at SoFi Stadium, essentially a neutral site, removes any significant home field advantage.

This is an intriguing stylistic matchup. Boise State will try to control tempo with their ground game while Washington looks to create explosive plays. The Broncos' experience in big bowl games gives them an edge in close situations.

Trojans @ Gamecocks

2025-12-17T02:00Z | Cramton Bowl | ESPN
TROY: 8-6 | JVST: 9-5

Two Sun Belt programs meet in the R+L Carriers Bowl at the historic Cramton Bowl in Alabama. Troy's 8-6 record includes some close losses, and the Trojans have the talent to compete with anyone in their conference. Their balanced offensive attack can be difficult to prepare for.

Jacksonville State went 9-5 in another strong season for the Gamecocks. Their transition to FBS continues to go smoothly, with a physical defense that causes problems and an efficient offense. They'll have home-state advantage with this game in Montgomery.

This is essentially a pick'em between two evenly matched programs. Jacksonville State's slight home-field edge and defensive toughness could be the difference, but Troy has the ability to pull off the upset with their passing game.

Monarchs @ Bulls

2025-12-17T22:00Z | Camping World Stadium | ESPN
ODU: 10-3 | USF: 9-4

Old Dominion had a fantastic season at 10-3 and earned their spot in the Cure Bowl at Camping World Stadium. The Monarchs have been one of the best stories in the Sun Belt, with a balanced attack and a defense that creates turnovers. They're playing with house money here.

South Florida's 9-4 campaign represents continued progress for the Bulls under their new coaching staff. The American Athletic Conference has been competitive, and USF has held their own. Their skill position players can make explosive plays when given space.

This should be an entertaining matchup between two programs on the rise. Old Dominion's physicality and ball security could be the deciding factors, but USF's home-state advantage in Orlando shouldn't be overlooked.

Cajuns @ Hens

2025-12-18T01:40Z | Hancock Whitney Stadium | ESPN
UL: 6-7 | DEL: 7-6

Louisiana limped to a 6-7 record in a disappointing season, but bowl eligibility was achieved and they'll look to end on a positive note. The Ragin' Cajuns have playmakers who can change games, but consistency has been their issue all year.

Delaware at 7-6 earned a bowl bid in their transition to FBS. The Blue Hens bring a physical CAA mentality and have surprised teams with their toughness. This is a program on the rise looking to make a statement against a Sun Belt opponent.

This is a tricky matchup for Louisiana. Delaware won't be intimidated and will play with tremendous effort. The Ragin' Cajuns have more talent on paper but motivation could be an issue after a frustrating regular season.

Bears @ Wolves

2025-12-19T02:00Z | Ford Center At The Star | ESPN2
MOST: 7-6 | ARST: 7-6

The Xbox Bowl at The Star in Frisco features two 7-6 teams looking to end on a high note. Missouri State made the leap from FCS and has shown they belong at this level. The Bears' physical running game and opportunistic defense have been their calling cards.

Arkansas State also finished 7-6 in the Sun Belt and brings experience to this matchup. The Red Wolves have been in this situation before and understand how to prepare for bowl games. Their balanced offensive attack can be difficult to defend.

Two evenly matched teams in a true toss-up game. This comes down to execution in key moments and which team can impose their will at the line of scrimmage. The venue at The Star is unique and should provide a great atmosphere.

Tide @ Sooners

2025-12-20T01:00Z | Memorial Stadium (Norman, OK) | ABC, ESPN
ALA: 11-3 | OU: 10-3

The College Football Playoff First Round brings a massive matchup between blue bloods. Alabama's 11-3 record includes some painful losses, but the Crimson Tide have the talent to beat anyone. Their quarterback play has been excellent, and the defense has shown flashes of vintage Bama dominance.

Oklahoma hosts at Memorial Stadium in what should be a raucous atmosphere. The Sooners went 10-3 in their first full SEC season, proving they belong among the elite. Their rushing attack has been physical, and the defense has found an identity under new coordinators.

This is exactly what the expanded playoff was designed for - two storied programs in a win-or-go-home setting. Alabama's experience in these moments is invaluable, but Oklahoma's home field advantage could be the great equalizer. Expect a physical, intense game decided by a possession or less.

Owls @ Broncos

2025-12-19T16:00Z | Brooks Stadium (SC) | ESPN
KENN: 10-4 | WMU: 10-4

The Myrtle Beach Bowl features two programs with identical 10-4 records. Kennesaw State has continued their successful transition from FCS, with an aggressive defense and a triple-option attack that creates headaches for opponents not used to defending it.

Western Michigan had an excellent MAC season and is battle-tested. The Broncos' balanced offense can attack through the air or on the ground, and their defensive front is disruptive. They've won five of their last six heading into this matchup.

This is an underrated bowl game with two quality teams. Kennesaw State's unique offensive system could give Western Michigan problems if they can't get stops, but the Broncos have enough offensive firepower to keep pace.

Dukes @ Ducks

2025-12-21T00:30Z | Autzen Stadium | TNT, HBO Max
JMU: 12-2 | ORE: 12-1

Another CFP First Round blockbuster. James Madison's 12-2 record represents one of the best seasons in program history under Bob Chesney. The Dukes are physical, well-coached, and believe they belong on any stage. Their defense creates turnovers and their offense controls the clock.

Oregon enters at 12-1 and played for the Big Ten championship. The Ducks' offense is explosive with elite speed at the skill positions, and their defense has improved significantly. Autzen Stadium is one of the toughest environments in college football, and it will be deafening.

This is a David vs Goliath matchup on paper, but JMU has earned respect with their resume. Oregon should have too much speed and depth, but the Dukes won't go down without a fight. Home field advantage at Autzen should be the deciding factor.

Wave @ Rebels

2025-12-20T20:30Z | Vaught-Hemingway Stadium | TNT, HBO Max
TULN: 11-3 | MISS: 12-1

The CFP First Round continues with Tulane traveling to Oxford. The Green Wave's 11-3 record includes an AAC championship, and they've proven they can play with anyone. Their physical offensive line and hard-nosed running game can control tempo and shorten games.

Ole Miss is 12-1 and enters as one of the hottest teams in the country. Lane Kiffin's offense is explosive and creative, while the defense has been much improved. At Vaught-Hemingway Stadium, the Rebels are extremely difficult to beat, with one of the best home-field advantages in college football.

Tulane will try to keep this close and make it a rock fight, but Ole Miss has too many weapons. The Rebels should pull away in the second half with their depth and explosiveness. The home crowd will be a factor throughout.

Hurricanes @ Aggies

2025-12-20T17:00Z | Kyle Field | ABC, ESPN
MIA: 11-2 | TA&M: 11-2

CFP First Round action at Kyle Field. Miami's 11-2 record caps a remarkable turnaround for the Hurricanes, with an offense that can put up points against anyone. The quarterback play has been elite, and the skill players are among the most explosive in the nation.

Texas A&M also went 11-2 and earned home field in the playoff. Kyle Field is one of the most intimidating environments in college football, and the 12th Man will be in full force. The Aggies' defense has been physical and their rushing attack methodical.

This is a fantastic matchup between two teams that can play multiple styles. Miami's passing attack versus A&M's run game creates an interesting chess match. Home field advantage at Kyle Field could be the deciding factor in what should be a tight contest.

Grizzlies @ Bobcats

2025-12-20T21:00Z | Bobcat Stadium (MT) | ABC
MONT: 13-2 | MTST: 13-2

The FCS Semifinals feature the Cat-Griz rivalry in what might be the best FCS game of the year. Montana's 13-2 record includes dominant performances throughout the playoffs, with a balanced offense and a stingy defense that's allowed just 15.8 points per game in the postseason.

Montana State is also 13-2 and has home field at Bobcat Stadium. This is one of the most hostile environments in all of college football, regardless of division. The Bobcats' rushing attack has been unstoppable, and their defense flies to the football.

The Brawl of the Wild in the FCS Semifinals - it doesn't get any better than this. Two programs with identical records, deep playoff experience, and a rivalry that means everything. Home field for Montana State could be the difference in an otherwise even matchup.

Redbirds @ Wildcats

2025-12-21T00:30Z | Villanova Stadium | ESPN2
ILST: 12-4 | VILL: 12-3

The other FCS Semifinal brings Illinois State to Villanova Stadium. The Redbirds are 12-4 and have been one of the most physical teams in the Missouri Valley Football Conference. Their rushing attack has been dominant in the playoffs, wearing down opponents with a relentless ground game.

Villanova's 12-3 record caps another excellent season for the CAA powerhouse. The Wildcats have championship pedigree and know how to win in December. Their defense has been outstanding, and the home crowd will provide a significant boost.

This is a classic FCS power matchup. Illinois State's ground game versus Villanova's stout defense creates a fascinating battle in the trenches. The team that establishes the run and controls possession will punch their ticket to the national championship game.
