2025-26 MLB Offseason

Hot Stove Alert

2025 World Series Champions: Los Angeles Dodgers

Dodgers defeat Yankees in 6 games | MVP: Shohei Ohtani

The Los Angeles Dodgers captured their second World Series title in four years, defeating the New York Yankees in six games. Shohei Ohtani delivered a historic postseason performance, becoming the first player to win World Series MVP as a designated hitter while hitting .400 with 5 home runs and 12 RBIs across the Fall Classic. The Dodgers rotation, anchored by Yoshinobu Yamamoto and Tyler Glasnow, held the vaunted Yankees lineup to just 3.2 runs per game.

Free Agency

Top Free Agents Available

2025-26 Offseason Class | Market Opens November 1

The 2025-26 free agent class features several impact players hitting the market. Juan Soto headlines the class after his monster year with the Yankees, while Corbin Burnes and Max Fried lead the pitching market. Other notable names include Pete Alonso, Anthony Santander, Teoscar Hernandez, and Blake Snell. The Mets, Cubs, Red Sox, and Giants are expected to be the most aggressive buyers this winter.

Biggest Names

Juan Soto (OF) - Expected to shatter records with 12+ year deal. Corbin Burnes (SP) - Elite command, premium market. Max Fried (SP) - Durable lefty ace type. Pete Alonso (1B) - 40+ HR power, polarizing defense. Blake Snell (SP) - Cy Young upside, health concerns.

Trade Rumors

Notable Trade Candidates

Teams looking to move salary or reset

Several teams are expected to be active on the trade market this winter. The White Sox continue their rebuild and could move any remaining veterans. The Marlins are open for business on nearly everyone. The Athletics, now in Sacramento, are looking to shed payroll before their Las Vegas move. Meanwhile, contenders like the Phillies, Astros, and Braves are looking to add impact pieces.

2025 Season Awards Predictions

Awards announced November 18-21

The major award races are set to be announced in late November. Shohei Ohtani is the overwhelming favorite for NL MVP after posting video game numbers in his first healthy season since joining the Dodgers. Aaron Judge and Bobby Witt Jr. are the frontrunners for AL MVP. The Cy Young races are tighter, with Chris Sale's comeback story competing against Tarik Skubal in the AL, and Zack Wheeler battling Dylan Cease in the NL.

Looking Ahead: 2026 Season Futures

Early odds and projections

The Dodgers open as favorites to repeat at +450, followed by the Yankees (+600), Phillies (+800), and Braves (+1000). The Padres and Orioles are popular sleeper picks after strong 2025 campaigns. On the flip side, the White Sox (+50000) and Athletics (+40000) project as the longest shots on the board heading into the offseason.
