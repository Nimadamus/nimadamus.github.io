Friday, November 28, 2025

Black Friday Rivalry Games

VS

#3 Texas A&M Aggies vs #16 Texas Longhorns - Lone Star Showdown

Friday 7:30 PM ET | ABC | DKR-Texas Memorial Stadium, Austin | A&M -2.5 | O/U 52.5

The Lone Star Showdown returns with massive stakes. The undefeated third-ranked Texas A&M Aggies bring an 11-0 record into Austin to face the 16th-ranked Texas Longhorns, who sit at 8-3. An A&M win clinches a spot in the SEC Championship Game for the first time ever. A Texas win keeps the Longhorns playoff hopes alive and continues their recent dominance in this rivalry.

The Line Movement Tells a Story

This line opened at Texas A&M -3 and has moved to -2.5, sometimes touching -2. Thats reverse line movement, indicating sharp money is backing the home dog. The Aggies are just 5-6 ATS this season despite being undefeated, while Texas is a brutal 3-8 ATS. However, Texas A&M is 1-4 SU in their last five games against Texas and 2-6 ATS in their last eight road games against the Longhorns. The under has hit in four of the last five meetings.

The Total Tells Another Story

The over has hit in five of Texas A&Ms last six games, but the under trend in this specific rivalry suggests defenses show up when bragging rights are on the line. The total of 52.5 feels about right for two teams that can score but also have the athletes to make stops in big moments.

VS

#7 Ole Miss Rebels vs Mississippi State Bulldogs - Egg Bowl

Friday 12:00 PM ET | ABC | Davis Wade Stadium, Starkville | Ole Miss favored

The Egg Bowl kicks off Black Friday with the seventh-ranked Ole Miss Rebels traveling to Starkville to face their in-state rivals. This game is always chaotic regardless of records, and Mississippi State will be desperate to play spoiler against an Ole Miss team with College Football Playoff aspirations. Lane Kiffins offense against a hostile Starkville crowd makes for appointment television.

Georgia Bulldogs vs Georgia Tech Yellow Jackets - Clean Old-Fashioned Hate

Friday 3:30 PM ET | ABC | Bobby Dodd Stadium, Atlanta | Georgia favored

Clean Old-Fashioned Hate brings Georgia to Atlanta to face Georgia Tech. The Bulldogs have dominated this rivalry recently, but rivalry games in the state of Georgia have a way of getting weird. Georgia Tech would love nothing more than to end their rivals season on a sour note, while Georgia needs to stay focused with bigger goals ahead.

Saturday, November 29, 2025

Championship Saturday Rivalry Games

VS

#1 Ohio State Buckeyes vs #17 Michigan Wolverines - THE GAME

Saturday 12:00 PM ET | FOX | Michigan Stadium, Ann Arbor | OSU -9.5 | O/U 44.5

This is it. The biggest game of college football Rivalry Week. The top-ranked Ohio State Buckeyes bring an 11-0 record into the Big House to face the 17th-ranked Michigan Wolverines, who are 9-2 and winners of five straight. Ohio State hasnt beaten Michigan in four years. Ryan Day is 0-4 against the Wolverines and desperately needs this win to quiet the critics. But Michigan owns this rivalry right now, and Sherrone Moore has the Wolverines believing they can make it five in a row.

The Spread Movement

This line has been wild. It opened at Ohio State -5.5 in May and climbed steadily as the Buckeyes dominated, peaking at -13.5 on Wednesday. Then the sharps struck. Professional bettors hammered Michigan +13.5, forcing the line back to -9.5 within 48 hours. That kind of movement screams smart money on the underdog.

The ATS Records

Ohio State is 9-2 ATS this season, covering at an elite rate. Michigan is just 4-7 ATS. But heres the thing: Ohio State is 1-4 ATS and 1-4 SU in their last five games against Michigan. The Wolverines have covered four straight in this rivalry. As of kickoff, Ohio State will have gone 2,192 days without beating Michigan, despite being favored by a touchdown or more in three of those four games. The low total of 44.5 reflects two elite defenses in a game where every possession matters.

VS

#10 Alabama Crimson Tide vs Auburn Tigers - Iron Bowl

Saturday 7:30 PM ET | ABC | Jordan-Hare Stadium, Auburn | Alabama -6 | O/U 47

The Iron Bowl heads to Jordan-Hare Stadium with the 10th-ranked Alabama Crimson Tide facing the Auburn Tigers. Alabama enters at 9-2 looking to finish the regular season strong, while Auburn sits at 5-6 and needs this win to become bowl eligible. The stakes are different for each team, but the Iron Bowl always delivers drama regardless of records.

Auburn's Home-Field Edge

Heres the trend that matters: Auburn is 5-1 ATS in their last six home games against Alabama. The Tide havent won a game at Auburn by more than three points since 2015. Thats a decade of close games at Jordan-Hare. Alabama is 7-4 ATS this season, while Auburn is just 4-7 ATS. But the under has hit in seven of Alabamas last eight games, suggesting this could be a defensive struggle.

Bowl Eligibility Motivation

Auburn needs this win to reach six wins and become bowl eligible. Thats a powerful motivator in a rivalry game. Alabama is the better team on paper, but laying six points on the road in the Iron Bowl has historically been a losing proposition.

Clemson Tigers vs South Carolina Gamecocks - Palmetto Bowl

VS

VS

Notre Dame Fighting Irish vs USC Trojans

Saturday 4:30 PM ET | NBC | LA Memorial Coliseum, Los Angeles

The greatest intersectional rivalry in college football returns with Notre Dame facing USC in Los Angeles. This game has decided national championships and ruined undefeated seasons for over a century. Both programs are looking to end their regular seasons on a high note, and this primetime matchup on NBC will have the entire country watching.
