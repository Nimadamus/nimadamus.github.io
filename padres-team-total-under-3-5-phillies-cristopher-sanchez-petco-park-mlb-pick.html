Cristopher Sanchez is in the best stretch of his season. He carries a 1.62 ERA, a 1.15 WHIP, and 86 strikeouts, and he has thrown four straight scoreless starts. That is not a soft spot for a San Diego lineup that now has to manufacture offense at the most pitcher-friendly park in baseball.
