Feast Week Tournament Action in Las Vegas

Monday, November 24, 2025

VS

#11 Alabama Crimson Tide vs #13 Gonzaga Bulldogs

Players Era Festival Las Vegas

Two top-15 programs clash in Las Vegas in one of the premier matchups of Feast Week. Alabama brings their explosive SEC offense against Gonzaga's balanced attack in what should be an entertaining showcase of elite college basketball.

VS

Tennessee Volunteers vs Rutgers Scarlet Knights

1:00 PM ET TNT Las Vegas

The Vols bring their SEC firepower to Vegas against a Big Ten opponent looking to make noise in non-conference play. Tennessee's athleticism and defensive intensity should overwhelm Rutgers in this early tipoff.

VS

Baylor Bears vs Creighton Bluejays

2:00 PM ET truTV

Two programs with Final Four aspirations clash in an early afternoon showcase. Baylor's physicality meets Creighton's shooting prowess in what should be a back-and-forth affair.

VS

Kansas Jayhawks vs Notre Dame Fighting Irish

3:30 PM ET TNT

Bill Self's Jayhawks look to establish early season momentum against a Notre Dame team seeking statement wins. Kansas traditionally dominates early season showcases with their depth and execution.

VS

St. John's Red Storm vs Iowa State Cyclones

4:30 PM ET truTV

Two tournament-caliber teams battle in what could be a preview of a March matchup. Iowa State brings their Big 12 toughness against St. John's Big East grit.

VS

Houston Cougars vs Syracuse Orange

6:00 PM ET TNT

Houston's suffocating defense faces Syracuse's zone in a chess match of defensive philosophies. The Cougars' disciplined approach should control tempo and limit Syracuse's transition opportunities.

VS

Oregon Ducks vs Auburn Tigers

8:00 PM ET truTV

Pac-12 meets SEC in an intriguing cross-conference battle. Auburn brings their trademark athleticism while Oregon counters with precise execution and three-point shooting.

VS

Michigan Wolverines vs San Diego State Aztecs

10:30 PM ET truTV

The late-night window features Michigan trying to establish their identity against a tough Mountain West opponent. San Diego State's defensive reputation makes them a formidable foe.
