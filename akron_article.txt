<p>Akron enters this game at 4-6 overall and 3-3 in MAC play, but those numbers don't tell the full story of their recent momentum. <p>But the real weapon for Akron is running back Jordan Gant, who leads the entire MAC with 946 rushing yards and 7 total touchdowns. <p>Think about that for a second. Kent State cannot run the football whatsoever. <p>But here's the problem: Kent State ranks second-to-last in third-down conversion rate at just 26.4%, which means they can't sustain drives.
