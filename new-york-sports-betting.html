Is Sports Betting Legal in New York?

Yes. Online sports betting became legal in New York on January 8, 2022. New York is now one of the largest legal sports betting markets in the United States with multiple licensed sportsbooks operating in the state.

You must be 21 years or older and physically located within New York state borders to place legal sports bets. All sportsbooks use geolocation technology to verify your location.

Best Sports to Bet On in New York

New York bettors have access to all major professional and college sports. The most popular betting options include:

NFL Football featuring the New York Giants and Buffalo Bills. The NFL dominates the New York betting market with massive handle on every game day.

NBA Basketball with the New York Knicks and Brooklyn Nets. Both teams generate huge betting interest, especially during playoff pushes.

MLB Baseball featuring the New York Yankees and New York Mets. Baseball betting is year round with daily games offering constant action.

NHL Hockey with the New York Rangers, New York Islanders, and Buffalo Sabres. Hockey betting is extremely popular in upstate New York.
