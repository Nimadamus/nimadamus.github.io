Tuesday's 8-Game NHL Slate: Cross-Conference Battles Heat Up

Posted: November 18, 2025

Tuesday night brings eight compelling NHL matchups spanning from 7 PM ET through the late 10 PM ET window. The slate features playoff contenders, intriguing road favorites, and some excellent home underdogs with value. Detroit welcomes Seattle as heavy home favorites, while the Rangers head to Vegas in a near pick'em, and Columbus travels to Winnipeg as the biggest underdog of the night.

SEATTLE @ DETROIT | 7:00 PM ET
@
Kraken @ Red Wings
Line: SEA +146 / DET -162 | Total: 6.0 | Public: 35% SEA / 65% DET

Detroit opens the evening as substantial home favorites with 65% public backing. The Red Wings are 11-7-1 overall with an impressive 6-3-1 home record and are solid at 11-8 ATS including 6-4 at home. Seattle comes in at 9-4-5 with a respectable 3-3-2 road record, posting a strong 14-4 ATS mark overall and 6-2 on the road. Both teams have been trending in opposite directions recently - Detroit is 5-4-1 in their last 10 while Seattle is 5-2-3. The over/under trends show Seattle at 5-4-1 O/U in their last 10 games compared to Detroit's 4-6 mark. The Kraken's elite ATS record on the road (6-2) provides value against the heavy public favorite.

ST. LOUIS @ TORONTO | 7:00 PM ET
@
Blues @ Maple Leafs | Consensus Picks: 2
Line: STL -102 / TOR -109 | Total: 6.0 | Public: 31% STL / 69% TOR

Two consensus picks on this near pick'em with Toronto getting heavy public backing at 69% at home. The Maple Leafs are 8-9-2 overall with a solid 7-4-2 home record but struggle ATS at 6-13 overall and 5-8 at home. St. Louis is struggling at 6-9-4 with a poor 3-4 road record and are equally bad ATS at 6-13 overall including 4-3 on the road. Toronto has been playing better lately at 4-5-1 in their last 10 compared to St. Louis' 3-4-3 mark. The total trends are interesting with Toronto going 9-1 over in their last 10 games while St. Louis is 5-5. Both teams are struggling to cover spreads, making this a difficult betting proposition despite the Leafs' home advantage.

NEW JERSEY @ TAMPA BAY | 7:00 PM ET
@
Devils @ Lightning | Consensus Picks: 1
Line: NJ +146 / TB -162 | Total: 5.5 | Public: 49% NJ / 51% TB

One consensus pick with the public nearly split on this Metropolitan vs. Atlantic matchup. The Devils come in red-hot at 13-4-1 overall with an impressive 6-4 road record and are 8-10 ATS including 4-6 on the road. Tampa Bay is 9-7-2 overall with a respectable 4-5 home record but struggles ATS at 7-11 overall and is particularly bad at home going 0-9 ATS. New Jersey is 6-3-1 in their last 10 games while Tampa is 7-3. The total trends show New Jersey at 5-5 O/U compared to Tampa's 4-6 mark in their last 10. Tampa's abysmal 0-9 home ATS record is the key stat here - they simply haven't covered as home favorites this season.

COLUMBUS @ WINNIPEG | 8:00 PM ET
@
Blue Jackets @ Jets | Consensus Picks: 1
Line: CLB +156 / WIN -174 | Total: 6.5 | Public: 26% CLB / 74% WIN

One consensus pick with Winnipeg drawing massive 74% public support at home. The Jets are 11-7 overall with a solid 5-3 home record and are 8-10 ATS including 4-4 at home. Columbus is 10-7-2 with an excellent 5-4-1 road record and is crushing it ATS at 12-7 overall including an elite 9-1 mark on the road. Both teams are 5-5 in their last 10 games, but Columbus is 5-4-1 O/U compared to Winnipeg's 6-4. The key trend here is Columbus' incredible 9-1 road ATS record - they've been consistently undervalued on the road and cover as big underdogs. The 74% public backing on Winnipeg creates even more value on Columbus.

NY ISLANDERS @ DALLAS | 8:00 PM ET
@
Islanders @ Stars | Consensus Picks: 2
Line: NYI +148 / DAL -164 | Total: 6.0 | Public: 31% NYI / 69% DAL

Two consensus picks with Dallas getting 69% public backing at home. The Stars are 12-4-3 overall with an excellent 6-3-1 home record but are brutal ATS at 8-11 overall and particularly bad at home going 2-8. The Islanders are 10-7-2 with a solid 6-4-1 road record and are 9-10 ATS including 7-4 on the road. Dallas is on fire lately at 7-1-2 in their last 10 while the Islanders are 6-3-1. The total trends show Dallas at 6-4 O/U compared to New York's 4-6 mark in their last 10. Dallas' 2-8 home ATS record is alarming - they consistently fail to cover as home favorites, making the Islanders' road ATS strength (7-4) an appealing contrarian angle.

CALGARY @ CHICAGO | 8:30 PM ET
@
Flames @ Blackhawks | Consensus Picks: 1
Line: CAL -115 / CHI +104 | Total: 6.0 | Public: 23% CAL / 77% CHI

One consensus pick with the public hammering Chicago at 77% despite being the home underdog. The Blackhawks are 9-5-4 overall with a solid 4-2-2 home record and are absolutely elite ATS at 15-3 overall including a perfect 7-1 at home. Calgary is struggling badly at 5-12-3 with a poor 2-8-1 road record and are 11-9 ATS overall including 5-6 on the road. Chicago is 5-3-2 in their last 10 while Calgary is 3-5-2. The total trends show Chicago at 4-5-1 O/U compared to Calgary's 4-5-1 mark. Chicago's 15-3 overall ATS record and 7-1 home ATS mark is one of the best in the NHL - they consistently cover as underdogs at home. The 77% public backing aligns with this elite trend.

UTAH @ SAN JOSE | 10:00 PM ET
@
Hockey Club @ Sharks | Consensus Picks: 1
Line: UTAH -131 / SJ +119 | Total: 6.0 | Public: 37% UTAH / 63% SJ

One consensus pick with the public backing the home underdog San Jose at 63%. Utah is 10-7-2 overall with a strong 5-6-1 road record but struggles ATS at 8-11 overall including 4-8 on the road. San Jose is 8-8-3 with a solid 4-3-3 home record and is crushing it ATS at 13-6 overall including an elite 8-2 mark at home. Utah is 3-5-2 in their last 10 while San Jose is 6-3-1. The total trends show San Jose at 3-7 under in their last 10 games compared to Utah's 5-5 mark. San Jose's 8-2 home ATS record is elite - they consistently cover as home underdogs. The public backing of 63% on the Sharks aligns with this strong trend.

NY RANGERS @ VEGAS | 10:00 PM ET
@
Rangers @ Golden Knights | Consensus Picks: 1
Line: NYR +115 / VEG -127 | Total: 6.0 | Public: 42% NYR / 58% VEG

One consensus pick closing out the slate with Vegas as a slight home favorite drawing 58% public support. The Golden Knights are 8-4-6 overall with a decent 4-3-3 home record but are struggling ATS at 6-12 overall including 2-8 at home. The Rangers are 10-8-2 with an outstanding 9-1-1 road record and are 10-10 ATS overall including an elite 8-3 road ATS mark. New York is 7-3 in their last 10 while Vegas is 3-3-4. The total trends show the Rangers at 3-7 under in their last 10 compared to Vegas' 4-6 mark. The Rangers' 9-1-1 road record and 8-3 road ATS mark is exceptional - they're one of the best road teams in the NHL. Vegas' 2-8 home ATS record creates significant value on the road underdog Rangers.

TUESDAY SLATE OVERVIEW

Tonight's eight-game slate features several compelling betting angles. The standout trends include Chicago's elite 15-3 overall ATS record (7-1 at home), San Jose's 8-2 home ATS mark, and Columbus' incredible 9-1 road ATS performance. On the flip side, Tampa Bay's 0-9 home ATS record and Dallas' 2-8 home ATS mark make them dangerous home favorites to back. The Rangers bring an outstanding 9-1-1 road record into Vegas, while Seattle's 6-2 road ATS performance provides value against the heavily-backed Detroit. Multiple games feature consensus picks including St. Louis-Toronto (2), NY Islanders-Dallas (2), and five others with single picks. The evening window from 7-8:30 PM ET is loaded with six games, while the late 10 PM ET window offers two compelling West Coast matchups. Key betting strategy: fade home favorites with poor home ATS records (Tampa 0-9, Dallas 2-8, Vegas 2-8) and back road teams with elite road ATS marks (Columbus 9-1, Rangers 8-3, Seattle 6-2).
