76ers @ Grizzlies

2025-12-31T01:00Z | FedExForum | NBC, Peacock
Line: PHI -1.5 | O/U: 234.5 | PHI: 16-14 | MEM: 15-17

Dude, the market's getting this completely wrong. Everyone's looking at Memphis being two games under .500 and thinking Philly's the easy play, but the Grizzlies have been quietly surging at home while the Sixers are falling apart on the road.

Memphis getting points at home against a dysfunctional road team? The value's screaming at you.

**Take: Grizzlies +1.5**
