Rice vs Texas State

Armed Forces Bowl | 1:00 PM ET | Fort Worth

All-Texas bowl affair at Amon G. Carter Stadium. Rematch of the 2023 First Responder Bowl when Texas State dominated 45-21. The Bobcats bring a physical rushing attack.

Navy vs Cincinnati

Liberty Bowl | 4:30 PM ET | Memphis

Navy at 10-2 continues the service academy bowl dominance. Military academies have won 7 straight bowl games since 2021. The Midshipmen's triple-option is nearly impossible to prepare for in bowl season.

Wake Forest vs Mississippi State

Duke's Mayo Bowl | 8:00 PM ET | Charlotte

ACC-SEC showdown under the lights in Charlotte. The Demon Deacons bring offensive creativity while the Bulldogs bring SEC physicality. Classic strength vs finesse matchup.

#17 Arizona vs SMU

Holiday Bowl | 8:00 PM ET | San Diego

Marquee matchup at Snapdragon Stadium. Arizona brings the #4 pass defense in the nation. SMU had an impressive first ACC year, beating Miami and Clemson. High-profile bowl with explosive offenses.
