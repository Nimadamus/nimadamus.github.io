La Liga: Rayo Vallecano vs Getafe

3:00 PM ET Estadio de Vallecas, Madrid ESPN Deportes, ESPN+

Madrid Derby

This is a proper Madrid derby between two of the capital city's smaller clubs. Rayo Vallecano and Getafe both play in the shadow of Real Madrid and Atletico, but their matches are always intense and physical. The Estadio de Vallecas is one of the most atmospheric grounds in La Liga, with Rayo's passionate fanbase creating an incredible atmosphere for these local derbies.

Rayo's Home Fortress

Rayo Vallecano has been difficult to beat at home this season. The Vallecas faithful create a wall of sound that intimidates visiting teams, and Rayo plays with incredible intensity in front of their supporters. They're a well-organized side that's difficult to break down, and they take points from better teams regularly.

Getafe's Physicality

Getafe is famous for their ultra-physical, defensive style of play. Jose Bordalas's sides are built to frustrate opponents, and they're happy to turn games into ugly, scrappy affairs. Getafe won't win any beauty contests, but they're effective at grinding out results, especially in these local derby matches.

This should be a tight, physical affair with both teams fighting for every ball. Rayo's home advantage gives them the edge, but Getafe's defensive organization could lead to a low-scoring draw. Expect under 2.5 goals and a battle for bragging rights in the Spanish capital.

Serie A: Cagliari vs AC Milan

2:45 PM ET Unipol Domus, Cagliari Paramount+

Rossoneri on the Road

AC Milan travels to Sardinia to face Cagliari in this Serie A clash. Milan is one of Italy's most storied clubs, and they'll be expected to leave with all three points. However, traveling to Cagliari is never easy. The island location and passionate home supporters make the Unipol Domus a difficult place for visiting teams.

Milan's Title Push

Milan has ambitions of challenging for the Scudetto this season, and they can't afford to drop points against teams like Cagliari. The Rossoneri have a talented squad with quality throughout the pitch, and they should have enough firepower to break down Cagliari's defense. Their attacking play has been impressive, and they create plenty of chances.

Cagliari's Survival Fight

Cagliari is fighting to avoid relegation, and every point matters in their survival battle. The Sardinian club has struggled this season, but they'll raise their game for a match against one of Serie A's giants. Home matches against the big clubs are opportunities to galvanize the fanbase and steal valuable points.

Milan should be too strong for Cagliari, but the away trip to Sardinia is always tricky. Expect Milan to win, but Cagliari could keep it closer than the odds suggest. The visitors should get the job done, but don't expect a blowout.

Ligue 1: Toulouse vs Lens

2:45 PM ET Stadium de Toulouse, Toulouse

French Football

Toulouse welcomes Lens to the Stadium de Toulouse in this Ligue 1 encounter. French football has been dominated by PSG in recent years, but clubs like Lens have been working to establish themselves as consistent challengers for European places. This should be an entertaining match between two well-organized sides.

Toulouse at Home

Toulouse has been solid at home this season, and the Stadium de Toulouse provides a good atmosphere for Ligue 1 matches. The home side will look to control possession and use their familiarity with the pitch to create chances. Toulouse plays attractive football and has the quality to compete with most teams in France.

Lens's European Ambitions

Lens has established themselves as one of France's better clubs outside of PSG. They have a passionate fanbase, excellent organization, and ambitions of qualifying for European competition. Lens plays direct, attacking football and is dangerous on the counter. They'll be confident of getting a result on the road.

This should be a competitive match with both teams creating chances. Lens's quality might be the difference, but Toulouse at home is no easy task. Expect goals and an entertaining affair. The draw could be value here if you think the teams will cancel each other out.

Saudi Pro League: Al Ahli vs Al Nassr

12:00 PM ET King Abdullah Sports City, Jeddah

Star-Studded Showdown

The Saudi Pro League has become a destination for world-class talent, and this match features some of the biggest names in football. Al Nassr has Cristiano Ronaldo, who continues to score goals at an incredible rate even in his late career. The Portuguese legend is determined to add more trophies to his collection, and Al Nassr is built around his talents.

Al Ahli's Star Power

Al Ahli isn't lacking in star power either. The Jeddah-based club has invested heavily in bringing top talent to Saudi Arabia, and they have the quality to compete with anyone in the league. Playing at home in King Abdullah Sports City gives them a significant advantage, and they'll be motivated to take down Ronaldo and Al Nassr.

Ronaldo Watch

Every Al Nassr match is appointment viewing because of Ronaldo. The 40-year-old shows no signs of slowing down, and he continues to produce moments of magic on a regular basis. His goal-scoring record in the Saudi Pro League has been phenomenal, and he'll be the focal point of Al Nassr's attack.

This is a marquee matchup in the Saudi Pro League, with both teams featuring global superstars. Al Nassr has Ronaldo, which always makes them dangerous, but Al Ahli at home has the quality to compete. Expect goals and entertainment in this clash between two of Saudi Arabia's best-funded clubs. Take the over in what should be a high-scoring affair.
