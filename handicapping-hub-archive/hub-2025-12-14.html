Stats Glossary

OFF RTG - Offensive Rating: Points scored per 100 possessions
DEF RTG - Defensive Rating: Points allowed per 100 possessions
NET RTG - Net Rating: OFF RTG minus DEF RTG
PACE - Possessions per 40 minutes
eFG% - Effective FG%: Adjusts for 3-pointers being worth more
TS% - True Shooting%: Accounts for FGs, 3s, and FTs
ATS - Against The Spread: Record covering point spreads
PWR - Power Rating: Team strength on 0-100 scale

STATS GLOSSARY

Understanding the advanced metrics shown in the Handicapping Hub:

BASKETBALL STATS

OFF RTG
Offensive Rating - Points scored per 100 possessions
DEF RTG
Defensive Rating - Points allowed per 100 possessions (lower is better)
NET RTG
Net Rating - OFF RTG minus DEF RTG (positive means outscoring opponents)
PACE
Possessions per 48 minutes - measures game tempo
eFG%
Effective Field Goal % - FG% adjusted for 3-pointers worth more
TS%
True Shooting % - Scoring efficiency including free throws
AST%
Assist Percentage - % of teammate field goals assisted
TOV%
Turnover % - Turnovers per 100 plays
OREB%
Offensive Rebound % - % of available offensive rebounds grabbed
DREB%
Defensive Rebound % - % of available defensive rebounds grabbed
PIE
Player Impact Estimate - Overall contribution metric (0.50 = league average)

BETTING TERMS

ATS
Against The Spread - Win/loss record when factoring in point spread
POWER RATING
Calculated team strength (70-100 scale, higher = better)
PUBLIC %
Percentage of bets on each side from recreational bettors
RLM
Reverse Line Movement - Line moves opposite of public betting (sharp indicator)
STEAM MOVE
Sudden, significant line movement indicating sharp action
SPREAD
Point handicap for betting purposes
O/U
Over/Under total points line
ML
Moneyline - Straight up winner odds

HOCKEY STATS

CF%
Corsi For % - Shot attempts for vs against (possession proxy)
xGF%
Expected Goals For % - Goal probability based on shot quality
PDO
Shooting % + Save % (100 = league average, regresses to mean)
GSAA
Goals Saved Above Average - Goalie performance vs average
SV%
Save Percentage - Shots saved divided by shots faced

FOOTBALL STATS

EPA
Expected Points Added - Value added on each play
DVOA
Defense-adjusted Value Over Average - Efficiency vs league average
PPG DIFF
Points per game differential (scored minus allowed)
RED ZONE %
Touchdown percentage when inside opponent's 20-yard line
