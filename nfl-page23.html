Expert analysis and betting picks for Thanksgiving Day triple-header

Thursday, November 27, 2025

Thanksgiving Day Triple-Header

@

Chicago Bears (4-8) @ Green Bay Packers (9-3)

4:25 PM ET Lambeau Field FOX

The oldest rivalry in the NFL. Bears vs. Packers at Lambeau Field in December is football tradition at its finest. Green Bay at 9-3 is having an excellent season under Matt LaFleur, with Jordan Love establishing himself as a franchise quarterback. The Packers are in prime position to win the NFC North.

Chicago's season has been a struggle at 4-8, but Caleb Williams continues to show the potential that made him the #1 overall pick. The rookie has had his ups and downs, but games against the Packers at Lambeau are where legends are made. Williams needs to show he can compete in the biggest rivalry in football. Green Bay should win, but these games are always competitive.
