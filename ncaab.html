Thursday, November 27, 2025

VS

#4 Duke Blue Devils vs #22 Arkansas Razorbacks

CBS Sports Thanksgiving Classic | 8:00 PM ET | CBS | United Center, Chicago | Duke -10.5 | O/U 157.5

This is the marquee game of Thanksgiving and one of the biggest nonconference matchups of the entire college basketball season. The fourth-ranked Duke Blue Devils bring a perfect 7-0 record into the United Center to face the 22nd-ranked Arkansas Razorbacks, who are 5-1 and winners of four straight.

Duke's Dominant Start

The Blue Devils have been absolutely suffocating opponents this season. Duke is scoring 93.6 points per game, ranking 16th nationally, while allowing just 57.0 points per contest, which ranks first in all of college basketball. Cameron Boozer has been the centerpiece, averaging over 21 points per game. Duke is 5-2 ATS this season and has gone UNDER in six of their last seven games.

Arkansas Looking to Make a Statement

The Razorbacks would love nothing more than to knock off a blue blood on the national stage. Meleek Thomas leads Arkansas with 18.3 points per game. Arkansas is 3-3 ATS this season and has gone 7-3 ATS in their last ten games overall. The UNDER has hit in four of their last five.

VS

#16 North Carolina vs #11 Michigan State

Fort Myers Tip-Off | 4:30 PM ET | FOX | Suncoast Credit Union Arena | MSU -1.5 | O/U 150.5

Two of college basketball's most storied programs clash in what could be an NCAA Tournament preview. Both North Carolina and Michigan State enter this game with perfect 6-0 records. The Spartans are narrow 1.5-point favorites, and Michigan State won the last meeting 94-91 in overtime.

The UNC Injury Situation

North Carolina has a massive frontcourt anchored by superstar freshman Caleb Wilson, who is averaging 20.5 points and 10.3 rebounds per game. The 7-foot center Henri Veesar adds 16.3 points and 8.7 boards. However, the Tar Heels lost senior guard Seth Trimble to a broken arm. This is the first time this season UNC is listed as a moneyline underdog.

Michigan State's Balanced Attack

The Spartans already have marquee wins over Arkansas and Kentucky this month. Point guard Jeremy Fears leads the entire nation with 10.2 assists per game. Michigan State is 9-3 SU and ATS against ranked opponents since the start of last season.

VS

#9 BYU Cougars vs Miami Hurricanes

5:00 PM ET | ESPN | State Farm Field House, Kissimmee | BYU -9.5 | O/U 164.5

The ninth-ranked BYU Cougars are heavy 9.5-point favorites against a Miami team that's been solid at 5-1 this season. BYU enters at 4-1 and looks to bounce back from their only loss. This game features the highest projected total on the Thanksgiving slate at 164.5 points.

BYU is 3-2 ATS this season and has a concerning trend: they are just 1-6 ATS in their last seven Thursday road games. Miami is 4-2 ATS and has covered in four of their last five. The OVER has hit in 8 of Miami's last 11 games.

VS

#10 Florida Gators vs TCU Horned Frogs

3:00 PM ET | FS1 | Jenny Craig Pavilion, San Diego | Florida -13.5 | O/U 155.5

The tenth-ranked Florida Gators are massive 13.5-point favorites against TCU in San Diego. Florida enters at 4-1 with a plus-17.0 scoring differential, while TCU is 3-2 and looking to pull off the upset of the day.

Florida is 0-3 ATS when favored by 13.5 points or more this season. Meanwhile, TCU is 5-0 ATS in their last five Thursday road games, and the Horned Frogs have covered in their last five overall. The UNDER has hit in 13 of TCU's last 20 games.

VS

VCU Rams vs #24 Vanderbilt Commodores

Battle 4 Atlantis | ESPN | Imperial Arena, Nassau, Bahamas

The Battle 4 Atlantis continues in the Bahamas with VCU taking on 24th-ranked Vanderbilt. The Commodores have been one of the surprises of the early season and look to add another quality win to their resume. VCU brings their trademark havoc defense to Nassau.

VS

Saint Mary's Gaels vs Virginia Tech Hokies

Battle 4 Atlantis | 2:30 PM ET | ESPN | Imperial Arena, Nassau | Saint Mary's -4.5

Saint Mary's continues their Bahamas trip as 4.5-point favorites against Virginia Tech. The Gaels are always a tough out in the nonconference season with their methodical, half-court offense and stingy defense. The winner gets a boost to their at-large resume.

VS

Oregon Ducks vs Creighton Bluejays

Players Era Festival | 2:00 PM ET | truTV | Michelob ULTRA Arena, Las Vegas | Creighton -1.5

The Players Era Festival continues in Las Vegas with Oregon and Creighton meeting in what should be an entertaining Big East vs Big Ten matchup. Creighton is a narrow 1.5-point favorite in this game featuring two programs with NCAA Tournament aspirations.

VS

UNLV Rebels vs Rutgers Scarlet Knights

Players Era Festival | 4:30 PM ET | truTV | Las Vegas | UNLV -4.5

UNLV is laying 4.5 points against Rutgers in Las Vegas. The Rebels have home-court advantage in their own city and look to continue building their Mountain West credentials.

VS

Georgetown Hoyas vs Dayton Flyers

ESPN Events Invitational | 7:30 PM ET | ESPN2 | Kissimmee, FL | Dayton -1.5

Georgetown and Dayton meet in the ESPN Events Invitational with Dayton as narrow 1.5-point favorites. The Flyers are always tough at neutral sites. This is essentially a pick'em game with two programs heading in different directions.

VS

Providence Friars vs Wisconsin Badgers

5:30 PM ET | FS1 | Jenny Craig Pavilion, San Diego | Wisconsin -6.5

Providence heads to San Diego to face Wisconsin, who is laying 6.5 points. The Badgers play their trademark slow, methodical style that can frustrate opponents, while Providence brings Big East toughness.
