Wednesday, November 26, 2025

Feast Week Tournament Action

Maui Invitational Championship, Battle 4 Atlantis Opening Round

Wednesday brings peak Feast Week action with multiple tournaments reaching critical stages. The Southwest Maui Invitational crowns a champion as Arizona State faces USC for the Wayne Duke Championship Trophy at the Lahaina Civic Center. The Battle 4 Atlantis tips off in the Bahamas with eight tournament-tested programs battling for supremacy. Add in the ESPN Events Invitational bracket games in Orlando, and college basketball fans have a smorgasbord of elite matchups spanning from Hawaii to the Caribbean to Florida.

The day's schedule runs from 11:00 AM ET with the Battle 4 Atlantis opener through the late-night Maui Invitational consolation games past midnight. This is the week when programs make statements, build resumes, and either validate or shatter preseason expectations. Every game has NCAA Tournament seeding implications. Every matchup tells us something about March contenders.

Maui Invitational Championship

vs

Arizona State Sun Devils vs USC Trojans

2:30 PM ET | ESPN | Lahaina Civic Center, Maui

Arizona State advanced to the 2025 Southwest Maui Invitational Championship Game with a thrilling 100-94 defeat of Washington State on Tuesday night. The Sun Devils (6-1) overcame a late-night game the previous day to pour in 100 points and clinch a spot in the finals. Their balanced offensive attack and aggressive style have been on full display in Maui, with multiple players contributing in crucial moments throughout the tournament.

USC earned their championship berth with a 70-67 victory over Boise State, demonstrating the gritty, defensive-minded identity that has defined their early season. The Trojans have shown they can win close games and execute in crunch time, which will be essential against an Arizona State team that pushes pace and creates chaos. This is a clash of styles with the Sun Devils wanting to run and the Trojans looking to grind. The championship trophy goes to whichever team can impose their will.

Maui Invitational Consolation Games

Seton Hall vs Washington State | Third Place | 5:30 PM ET | ESPN2

Seton Hall reached the third-place game after upsetting No. 23 NC State 85-74 in the opening round, one of the biggest surprises of Feast Week. The Pirates have proven they can compete with ranked opponents and will look to close their Maui trip with a statement victory. Washington State fell to Arizona State in a high-scoring semifinal and brings a potent offense that can light up the scoreboard. This projects as an entertaining consolation matchup with both teams playing for resume-building wins.

NC State vs TBD | Fifth Place | 9:30 PM ET | ESPN2

The 23rd-ranked Wolfpack look to salvage their Maui trip after an upset loss to Seton Hall. NC State bounced back with an 81-70 victory over Boise State on Tuesday, demonstrating the resilience that made them a Final Four team last March. This is a crucial game for the Wolfpack's national perception heading into ACC play.

Battle 4 Atlantis Opening Round

vs

#24 Vanderbilt Commodores vs Western Kentucky Hilltoppers

12:00 PM ET | ESPNU | Atlantis Paradise Island, Bahamas

No. 24 Vanderbilt opens as the favorite to win the Battle 4 Atlantis championship, and their tournament run begins against the Western Kentucky Hilltoppers. The Commodores bring SEC pedigree and elite athleticism to the Bahamas, while WKU represents Conference USA with their trademark physical style. Vanderbilt's depth and versatility should control this opener, but the Hilltoppers won't go quietly.

Four of the eight teams in this year's Battle 4 Atlantis field played in the 2025 NCAA Tournament, raising the stakes for every game. The winner advances to face the Colorado State-Virginia Tech winner in Thursday's semifinals. Vanderbilt needs a strong showing in this prestigious event to cement their status as a legitimate SEC contender and Final Four threat.

vs

Virginia Tech Hokies vs Colorado State Rams

5:00 PM ET | ESPN2 | Atlantis Paradise Island, Bahamas

Virginia Tech meets Colorado State in what projects as one of the more competitive opening-round matchups. The Hokies bring their ACC brand of basketball to the Bahamas, featuring versatile guards and a physical defensive approach. Colorado State represents the Mountain West with an experienced roster and the ability to control pace.

This is a pivotal game for both programs' non-conference resumes. The winner gets a potential matchup with No. 24 Vanderbilt in the semifinals, while the loser drops to the consolation bracket. Virginia Tech should be slight favorites given their conference affiliation, but Colorado State has the veterans to pull the upset in a neutral-site environment.

vs

Wichita State Shockers vs Saint Mary's Gaels

6:30 PM ET | ESPN2 | Atlantis Paradise Island, Bahamas

Wichita State and Saint Mary's clash in a matchup of mid-major powers with contrasting styles. The Gaels bring their trademark Randy Bennett system: methodical pace, elite shooting, and disciplined defense. Saint Mary's has consistently produced NCAA Tournament teams through player development and tactical precision. Wichita State counters with AAC athleticism and the Shocker fan base's historic passion for early-season tournament play.

This is the game that could produce the tournament's Cinderella story if Wichita State can match Saint Mary's execution. The Gaels are favorites based on their consistency and proven system, but the Shockers have the talent to pull an upset. The winner faces the Vanderbilt-WKU winner path, creating a potential semifinal showdown between mid-major darlings and power conference depth.

vs

VCU Rams vs South Florida Bulls

2:30 PM ET | ESPN2 | Atlantis Paradise Island, Bahamas

VCU and South Florida round out the opening-round action in the Battle 4 Atlantis. The Rams bring their trademark havoc defense and up-tempo style that has made them a perennial mid-major power. South Florida represents the AAC with their athletic roster and looks to make a statement on the national stage. This game determines who faces the Wichita State-Saint Mary's winner in Thursday's semifinals.

ESPN Events Invitational

Temple vs Rhode Island | 7:30 PM ET | ESPNU | Orlando, FL

Temple and Rhode Island meet in their final game of the ESPN Events Invitational's Adventure bracket. Rhode Island enters following a 62-55 loss to Towson and an 80-65 win over Vermont, showing the inconsistency that has defined their early season. Temple brings AAC firepower to this matchup, looking to close their tournament run with a statement victory.

Both teams are fighting for positioning in mid-major conference races, and a strong finish in Orlando can build momentum heading into league play. The ESPN Events Invitational has provided valuable resume-building opportunities for both programs, and this final game determines placement in the Adventure bracket standings.
