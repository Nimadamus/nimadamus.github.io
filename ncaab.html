The Matchup

This is what's known as a "buy game" - a scheduling cupcake for a ranked team. Iowa State at 10-0 is one of the best teams in the country, ranked in the top 5 and a legitimate Big 12 title contender. Eastern Illinois at 2-7 is an Ohio Valley Conference team getting a nice paycheck to come to Hilton Coliseum and take a beating. The Panthers are averaging just 68 points per game and allowing 78 - that's a negative-10 point differential against mediocre competition. Iowa State, meanwhile, has been winning by an average of 22 points per game.

The Lines

41 points is an absurd spread, but that's what happens when a top-5 team hosts a bottom-tier OVC squad. Iowa State has been crushing inferior opponents all season - they beat North Alabama by 31, Lipscomb by 27, and Jackson State by 25. Eastern Illinois is not better than any of those teams. The 144.5 total suggests the Cyclones will put up around 90+ while holding EIU to 50-55.

The Angle

Here's the question: can Iowa State cover 41? That means winning by 42+. Even dominant teams struggle to cover 40+ spreads because they pull starters in the second half and let the bench mop up. Iowa State coach T.J. Otzelberger typically rests his guys once a game is out of reach. If the starters sit with 10 minutes left, the spread is in jeopardy. If you're betting this, you're betting Iowa State keeps their foot on the gas or Eastern Illinois is so bad that even the bench can blow them out.
