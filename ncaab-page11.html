23-Game Slate - Expert Analysis & Predictions

Friday, November 28, 2025

vs

#22 Indiana Hoosiers vs Minnesota Golden Gophers

7:00 PM ET Big Ten Network

Big Ten basketball is back in full swing, and this conference matchup between the #22 Indiana Hoosiers and Minnesota Golden Gophers is going to set the tone for the rest of the league season. Assembly Hall is one of the most iconic venues in college basketball, and the Hoosiers feed off that energy like no other team.

Indiana has been playing excellent basketball this season, earning their #22 ranking through consistent effort and strong team play. The Hoosiers have the depth to wear down opponents, they defend with intensity, and they execute in the half court when they need to. This is a program that knows how to win big games, especially at home.

Minnesota's trying to build something in the Big Ten, and road games against ranked opponents are opportunities to prove they belong. The Golden Gophers have enough talent to hang in this game, but the margin for error is small. One bad stretch, one poorly executed possession, and Indiana can capitalize and pull away.

Here's the angle: Indiana at home in a Big Ten game is dangerous for any opponent. The crowd at Assembly Hall creates an atmosphere that genuinely affects outcomes. Minnesota's going to have to handle the pressure, deal with the noise, and still execute their game plan. That's a tall order for any team.

Assembly Hall's atmosphere creates a genuine home court advantage that affects outcomes. Indiana's #22 ranking, their depth, and their defensive intensity versus Minnesota's road challenges in a hostile Big Ten environment creates separation. The Hoosiers' experience in big games at home is a notable factor in this conference matchup.

vs

#20 Auburn Tigers vs NC State Wolfpack

9:15 PM ET ESPN2

Auburn basketball under Bruce Pearl is always appointment television. The Tigers play with a tenacity and energy that makes them must-watch, and their #20 ranking reflects a program that's competing at the highest level in the SEC. This team knows how to win games, and they're going to bring that intensity against NC State tonight.

The Tigers have the athleticism and depth to overwhelm opponents. Pearl's system demands maximum effort on every possession, and his players buy in completely. Auburn crashes the offensive glass, they pressure the ball full court, and they create turnovers that lead to easy baskets in transition. When everything's clicking, the Tigers are one of the toughest outs in college basketball.

NC State comes in as an underdog, but this is a proud ACC program that won't back down. The Wolfpack have enough experience to compete in big games, and they'll try to slow the pace and make this a half-court battle. If NC State can control tempo and limit Auburn's transition opportunities, they've got a chance to keep this competitive.

Here's what I like about Auburn in this spot: they're the more athletic team, they're playing with confidence, and they have the coaching edge with Bruce Pearl on the sideline. Pearl's teams always show up ready for big games, and while NC State is a quality opponent, the Tigers should have too much firepower.

Auburn's athleticism, depth, and Bruce Pearl's coaching create significant advantages. The Tigers' full-court pressure, offensive rebounding, and transition game versus NC State's need to control tempo sets up a compelling contrast in styles. Auburn's SEC experience and Pearl's track record in big games are noteworthy factors.

vs

#17 Vanderbilt Commodores vs SMU Mustangs

9:15 PM ET ESPNU

Vanderbilt basketball is having a moment, and the #17 ranking proves it. The Commodores have been one of the surprise stories of college basketball this season, playing with a confidence and cohesion that's elevated them into the national conversation. This is a program that's learned how to win, and they're dangerous.

SMU is adjusting to life in the ACC, and it's been a learning experience. The Mustangs have talent, but competing against Power Five competition night after night is different than the American Athletic Conference. They're going to be tested tonight against a ranked Vanderbilt team that's playing excellent basketball.

Here's what makes Vanderbilt dangerous: they play smart basketball. They don't beat themselves with turnovers, they execute in the half court, and they defend with purpose. The Commodores aren't going to outathlete you, but they're going to outthink you and outexecute you. That's a tough combination to beat.

The key for SMU is playing to their strengths and not getting caught up in Vanderbilt's pace. The Commodores want to control tempo, grind possessions, and make every trip down the floor count. If SMU can speed things up and create chaos, they've got a chance. If Vanderbilt dictates the pace, this gets difficult for the Mustangs.

Vanderbilt's #17 ranking reflects consistent excellent play and smart basketball. The Commodores' low turnover rate, half-court execution, and defensive purpose versus SMU's adjustment to ACC competition creates an interesting dynamic. Vanderbilt's ability to control tempo and outexecute opponents has been their calling card this season.

vs

#9 BYU Cougars vs Cal Baptist Lancers

9:00 PM ET ESPN+

BYU at #9 in the nation is playing some of the best basketball in program history. The Cougars have been dominant this season, and their ranking reflects a team that's competing at the highest level. This is a squad that can beat anybody, and they're proving it week after week.

Cal Baptist is an overmatched opponent in this one. The Lancers are competitive in their conference, but facing a top-ten team like BYU is a different animal entirely. The athleticism gap, the depth gap, the experience gap, it's all going to show up tonight. This is the kind of game where BYU should dominate from start to finish.

Here's what I expect: BYU comes out aggressive, establishes their pace early, and builds a comfortable lead by halftime. The Cougars have too much talent to let a mid-major hang around for forty minutes. They're going to impose their will, execute their system, and make this look easy.

The only question is how many points BYU wins by. Cal Baptist will compete hard, they'll make some shots, and they won't quit. But the talent disparity is significant, and BYU should cruise to a comfortable victory.

The talent disparity is significant in this matchup. BYU's #9 ranking represents some of the best basketball in program history, while Cal Baptist faces a major step up in competition from their usual conference games. The athleticism gap, depth gap, and experience gap all favor the Cougars substantially in this one.
