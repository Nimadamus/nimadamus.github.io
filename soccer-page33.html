The Vitality Stadium has proven a tricky venue for even the biggest sides, and Bournemouth's compact home ground creates an intensity that can unsettle visitors accustomed to more spacious surroundings. The Cherries have struggled recently, winning just one of their last fourteen matches, but their home form remains marginally more respectable than their disastrous away record. Andoni Iraola's pressing system can cause problems for teams trying to build from the back, though sustaining that energy for ninety minutes against Liverpool's quality is another matter entirely.
