2025 Postseason Results

World Series Champion: Los Angeles Dodgers

The Los Angeles Dodgers captured their second World Series title in three years, defeating the New York Yankees in six games. Shohei Ohtani was named World Series MVP after hitting .385 with 3 home runs and 10 RBI in the Fall Classic.

ALCS: Yankees def. Astros (4-2)

The Yankees advanced to their first World Series since 2009, ending Houston's dynasty with a clutch Aaron Judge home run in Game 6.

NLCS: Dodgers def. Phillies (4-3)

An instant classic series went the distance, with the Dodgers winning Game 7 at home on a walk-off single by Mookie Betts.
