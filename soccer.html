5 Premier League Matches - Chelsea vs Arsenal London Derby

Sunday, November 30, 2025

Chelsea vs Arsenal

4:30 PM GMT / 11:30 AM ET Premier League Stamford Bridge Sky Sports / USA Network

The biggest match of the Premier League weekend brings Arsenal to Stamford Bridge for a London derby with huge title implications. Arsenal entered the weekend leading the table, while Chelsea is hoping to close the gap on the Gunners. Cole Palmer is fit and available to start after returning from a groin injury that sidelined him since September, giving Chelsea a major boost for this clash.

Arsenal's Title Push

Martin Odegaard is back after injury, though he may start from the bench. The Norwegian captain has seven goal involvements against Chelsea - his best return against any Premier League opponent - including setting up both Arsenal goals against the Blues last season. Arsenal will be without Gabriel due to injury, with Cristhian Mosquera stepping in alongside William Saliba. Eberechi Eze has been sensational in midfield, scoring a hat trick against Spurs last weekend.

Chelsea's Young Stars

Teenage sensation Estevao Willian has been electric for Chelsea, scoring another goal in midweek against Barcelona in the Champions League. Manager Enzo Maresca faces a selection dilemma - it's hard to leave Estevao out after his recent performances. The combination of Palmer's return and Estevao's form gives Chelsea multiple weapons to trouble Arsenal's defense. This should be an entertaining, open game between two attack-minded sides.

West Ham United vs Liverpool

2:05 PM GMT / 9:05 AM ET Premier League London Stadium Peacock / NBCSN

Liverpool travel to the London Stadium looking to extend their advantage at the top of the table. Mohamed Salah was named November's Premier League Player of the Month after scoring four goals and providing an assist as Arne Slot's men opened up their lead. His winner against Brighton, clinching strike vs Aston Villa (where he became only the third player in club history to reach 250 Liverpool goals, after Ian Rush and Roger Hunt), and vital brace at Southampton showcased his continued brilliance.

Title Defense

Liverpool are the defending Premier League champions after their title last season, and Salah signed a two-year extension in April 2025, committing him to the club until 2027. His 2024-25 season saw him finish as the league's top scorer with 29 goals and 18 assists - 47 goal involvements, the highest total ever in a 38-game season. West Ham will need to be at their best to contain the Egyptian King.

Crystal Palace vs Manchester United

12:00 PM GMT / 7:00 AM ET Premier League Selhurst Park TNT Sports

The day's action kicks off at Selhurst Park as Crystal Palace hosts Manchester United. Selhurst Park is always a difficult venue for visiting teams, with the atmosphere and Palace's direct style creating problems for many opponents. United will look to build consistency in the league as they navigate a transitional period.

Additional Sunday Matches

Aston Villa vs Wolves Nottingham Forest vs Brighton 2:05 PM GMT

Two more matches kick off at 2:05 PM GMT alongside the Liverpool game. Aston Villa hosts Wolves in a Midlands derby at Villa Park - both matches were moved due to Villa's Champions League participation on Thursday. Nottingham Forest takes on Brighton at the City Ground, with Forest looking to continue their impressive European form in domestic competition after strong Europa League performances.
