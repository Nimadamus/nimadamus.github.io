Saturday's Loaded 13-Game NHL Slate: Cross-Conference Action Heats Up

Posted: November 15, 2025

Saturday night delivers one of the biggest slates of the young season with 13 NHL matchups spanning both conferences. We've got everything from Atlantic Division battles to Western Conference showdowns, with several games featuring significant public betting splits and intriguing matchups. The board is loaded from 5 PM ET through the late West Coast window at 10 PM ET.

TAMPA BAY @ FLORIDA | 5:00 PM ET
@
Lightning @ Panthers
Line: TB +118 / FLA -130 | Total: 6.0 | Public: 32% TB / 68% FLA

The early game features a Florida team that's heavily favored at home against Tampa Bay. The Panthers are 9-7-1 overall with a solid 6-1-1 home record, while Tampa sits at 8-6-2 with a respectable 4-2-2 road mark. The public is hammering Florida at 68%, but Tampa has covered at a 6-2 clip on the road this season. Both teams are playing well lately with 7-3 and 6-3-1 records in their last 10 games respectively. The total of 6.0 has seen movement with -130 on the over.

ANAHEIM @ MINNESOTA | 6:00 PM ET
@
Ducks @ Wild
Line: ANA +113 / MIN -125 | Total: 6.5 | Public: 57% ANA / 43% MIN

Interesting split here with the public actually favoring the road underdog Anaheim at 57%. The Ducks come in at 11-5-1 with a strong 6-4-1 road record and have been hot lately at 7-3 in their last 10. Minnesota is 7-7-4 overall but just 4-3-3 at home. The Wild have struggled against the spread at 7-11 overall and are particularly bad at home at 2-8 ATS. Anaheim is 10-7 ATS this season including 6-5 on the road. Both teams have been trending over with 6-4 O/U marks in their last 10 games.

TORONTO @ CHICAGO | 7:00 PM ET
@
Maple Leafs @ Blackhawks | Consensus Picks: 3
Line: TOR -115 / CHI +104 | Total: 6.5 | Public: 40% TOR / 60% CHI

This one has drawn three consensus picks and features the public backing the home underdog Chicago at 60%. The Blackhawks are 8-5-4 overall with a 3-2-2 home record, but their 14-3 home ATS record is absolutely elite. Toronto is 8-8-2 but has been dreadful on the road at 1-4 with a 1-4 ATS mark. The Leafs have been an over machine with a 10-0 O/U record in their last 10 games. Chicago is 4-5-1 O/U in that span. Toronto is 6-12 ATS overall, while Chicago is crushing it at 14-3 ATS overall.

@
Bruins @ Canadiens | Consensus Picks: 1
Line: BOS +138 / MON -153 | Total: 6.0 | Public: 52% BOS / 48% MON

Montreal is 10-5-2 with a solid 5-3-1 home mark but just 7-10 ATS overall. Boston has been on fire lately at 8-2 in their last 10 and is 7-3 O/U in that span. Montreal is 5-3-2 in their last 10 and trending heavily over at 9-1 O/U. The public is nearly split with a slight 52% lean toward Boston.

@

Washington is 8-8-1 with a 4-4 home record and 9-8 ATS overall. The Devils are 6-3-1 in their last 10 while the Capitals are 3-6-1. SATURDAY SLATE OVERVIEW

This massive 13-game slate offers something for everyone. The key trends to watch include several home favorites drawing heavy public support (Florida 68%, Carolina 71%, Detroit 73%, Dallas 72%), while some road teams like Anaheim and the Rangers have been exceptional ATS. Toronto's perfect 10-0 over record in their last 10 games stands out, as does Chicago's elite 14-3 home ATS mark. Buffalo's winless road record (0-5-2) makes them a fade candidate against Detroit. Several games feature consensus picks including Toronto-Chicago (3), Vegas-St. Louis (2), and Winnipeg-Calgary (2). The evening window from 7-8 PM ET is particularly loaded with seven games, giving bettors plenty of options to choose from across multiple time slots.
