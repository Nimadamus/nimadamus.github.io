Packers @ Lions

2025-12-05T01:15Z | Ford Field | Prime Video
Line: DET -6 | O/U: 51.5 | GB: 8-4 | DET: 10-2

This is the game of the week, and it's not even close. The Lions sit atop the NFC at 10-2 and have been absolutely demolishing opponents lately, winning their last four games by an average of 21 points. Jared Goff is playing the best football of his career, completing 72% of his passes with 24 touchdowns and just 4 interceptions. The supporting cast is ridiculous - Amon-Ra St. Brown, Jameson Williams, and Sam LaPorta give Detroit three legitimate weapons at every level.

But don't sleep on Green Bay. Jordan Love has taken a massive leap in his second full season as a starter, throwing for 3,100 yards with 26 touchdowns. The Packers are 8-4 and very much in the wild card hunt. Romeo Doubs has emerged as a true number one receiver, and Josh Jacobs has been a revelation since coming over from Las Vegas. Green Bay's defense has been surprisingly stout, ranking 8th in points allowed.

The Lions are 6-0 at Ford Field and the crowd will be absolutely electric for this Thursday night showdown. Detroit's run game with David Montgomery and Jahmyr Gibbs has been impossible to stop, averaging 162 rushing yards per game. Green Bay will need to find a way to slow down that ground attack or this could get ugly. The over has hit in 7 of Detroit's last 9 games, and both of these offenses can score in bunches.
