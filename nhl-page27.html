Canadiens @ Lightning

2025-12-28T22:00Z | Benchmark International Arena | ESPN+, The Spot - MTN
Line: TB -148 | O/U: 6.5 | MTL: 20-12-5 | TB: 21-13-3

Tampa's got a chip on their shoulder after getting bounced early last playoffs, and they're not letting teams waltz into their building like Montreal did earlier this season. The Habs embarrassed them 5-2 back in November, and Lightning players were chirping about it for weeks afterward.

Both teams sitting at 45 points creates natural intensity, but Tampa's depth advantage shows up in these revenge spots. Tampa's four-line rotation grinds teams down, especially at home where they control matchups.

This total feels inflated given both teams' recent defensive improvements, but the revenge factor pushes this toward a higher-scoring affair than expected.

**Take: Tampa -148 and hammer the over 6.5**
