Smartest Bets in the Casino

These are the sharpest, lowest-house-edge plays you can make inside a casino. Ranked from best to worst — based on house edge — these are the bets that give you the best chance to beat the house, or at least lose the slowest.

#1 – Blackjack (Card Counting, 3:2 Single or Double Deck)

House Edge: As low as -1.0% (player edge if counting effectively)

Tip: Learn a card counting system like Hi-Lo and stick to tables with favorable rules: 3:2 payout, double after split, late surrender.

#2 – Baccarat (Betting Banker)

House Edge: ~1.06%

Tip: Avoid tie and dragon bets, they have a massive house edge.

#3 – Craps (Don't Pass Line)

House Edge: ~1.36%

Tip: Combine Don't Pass with full odds behind the line to keep your edge minimal. Avoid proposition bets.

#4 – Video Poker (9/6 Jacks or Better)

House Edge: ~0.46% (with perfect strategy)

Tip: Only play full-pay machines (9/6 payout) and study the proper strategy chart.

#5 – Pai Gow Poker

House Edge: ~1.5%

Tip: This is a slow-paced game with lots of pushes, making it ideal for reducing hourly losses. Bank as often as possible.
