What Is This Page?

This page shows betting consensus data - the percentage of bets placed on each side of a game. This data helps you understand where the general public ("squares") is betting.

How to use this information:

  • High percentage (65%+) on one side = Heavy public action. Some bettors like to "fade the public" and bet the other side.
  • Look for value = When the public hammers one side, the line may move, creating value on the less popular side.
  • Not a guarantee = The public wins sometimes too. Use this as ONE tool in your analysis, not the only factor.

Data updates hourly. Percentages show % of total bets on each side.

Understanding the Data

SPREAD % Percentage of bets on each team to cover the point spread. Higher % = more public money on that side.
O/U % Percentage of bets on the Over vs Under for the total points. Shows public lean on game tempo.
ML % Percentage of bets on each team's moneyline (to win straight up). Often favors favorites.
Highlighted Numbers The side with more bets is highlighted in blue. 65%+ triggers a "potential fade" alert.
